Information About Me

My journey as a game developer began through the lens of the player . Reaching diamond rank in League at 14 years old kickstarted my passion for competitive gaming, motivating me to work towards high ranks in other games. My notable performances arose from Distinguished Master Guardian (CS:GO) to Top 500 (Overwatch) to Diamond (Apex Legends), and ultimately, Immortal (Valorant).* Being an avid player gave me an appreciation for the intentions and details developers apply to create exciting projects. Naturally, game development became the vessel through which I can encompass and expand on the fulfilling experiences curated by past developers.
